Butter Mochi

About

  • Recipe copied from Hawaiian Electric’s page for posterity.
  • Demonstrated by: Helen Kuoha-Torco.
  • Aloha Festivals - August 16, 2003.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb mochiko (Japanese glutinous rice flour)
  • 3 cups sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 4 eggs, beaten
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup butter, melted
  • 1 can (13.5 oz) coconut milk
  • 1 can (14.5 oz) evaporated milk

Directions:

  1. Preheat electric oven to 350Â°F.
  2. Grease a 13 x 9 x 2-inch pan or two 8 x 8 x 2-inch pans.
  3. In a large bowl, combine mochiko, sugar, and baking powder.
  4. Add remaining ingredients and mix well with a wire whisk.
  5. Pour into prepared pan(s) and bake for 50 to 60 minutes then let cool.
  6. Makes 24 servings.
